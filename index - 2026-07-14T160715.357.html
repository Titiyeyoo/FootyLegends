Four ways to play with them.

Draw 30 legends. Build your XI.
LOCK IN to reveal the story you made.

Your own England XI. Any player, any era.
Start empty, get surprised, or use a story you've unlocked.

what kind of fan you really are.

No sign-up. No accounts. We don't collect your name, email or any personal data.
